Prologue

College is a place for finding answers. For young adults to figure out what they want to do with their life, study that field, and meet people who can help you in that field. Though these answers come to ssome people rather quickly, there are still those who lack an idea of what they want to do. Enter Conner O'donnell. A college sophmore who lacks any direction, and is in a race to figure out his goals.
